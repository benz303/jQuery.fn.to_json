Jquery.fn.to_json

This is a jQuery plugin for convert form elements to json.

$('<div><input name="a" value="a" /></div>').to_json()
  //=> {a: 'a'}
$('<div><input name="a.b" value="b" /></div>').to_json()
  //=> {a: {b: 'b'}}
$('<div><input name="a.b[]" value="1" /><input name="a.b[]" value="2" /></div>').to_json()
  //=> {a: {b: ['1', '2']}}
